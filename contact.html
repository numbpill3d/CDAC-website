What to Expect

For Equipment Donations: We will contact you to discuss what equipment you have available, schedule a convenient pickup time, and provide documentation for your records.

For Device Requests: We will ask about your specific needs and circumstances. Once we have equipment that matches your requirements, we will reach out to coordinate distribution.

For General Inquiries: We are happy to answer questions about our process, partnership opportunities, volunteer positions, or anything else related to our mission.
