Technology Should Be Accessible to Everyone

We refurbish donated computer equipment and provide it to students, learners, and communities who need it most. Together, we can keep functional hardware out of landfills and in the hands of people who need it.

Donate Equipment Today

Our Mission

Every piece of technology has the potential to change a life

The Carolina Digital Access Coalition refurbishes donated computer equipment and redistributes it to students, learners, and communities without reliable access to technology. We believe functional hardware shouldn't end up in landfills when it could end up in hands. Based in Charlotte, NC, we accept equipment donations regionally and nationwide.

Equipment We Accept

  • Business-class laptops (ThinkPad, Dell Latitude, HP EliteBook)
  • Desktop computers
  • Monitors and displays
  • Keyboards, mice, and peripherals
  • Networking equipment

Our Process

  • Secure data wiping (DOD 5220.22-M standard)
  • Hardware diagnostics and repair
  • Operating system installation
  • Quality testing and verification
  • Distribution to qualified recipients

Who We Help

  • Students from low-income families
  • Community learning centers
  • Nonprofit organizations
  • Job training programs
  • Seniors and adult learners
